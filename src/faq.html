FAQs for Mentee's

 What is PVTG?
75 tribal groups have been categorized by the Ministry of Home Affairs as Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTG)s. PVTGs reside in 18 States and UT of A&N Islands. The Ministry of Tribal Affairs implements the Scheme of “Development of Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs)” exclusively for them . Under the scheme, Conservation-cum-Development (CCD)/Annual Plans are to be prepared by each State/UT for their PVTGs based on their need assessment, which are then appraised and approved by the Project Appraisal Committee of the Ministry. Activities for development of PVTGs are undertaken in Sectors of Education, Health, Livelihood and Skill Development , Agricultural Development, Housing & Habitat, and Conservation of Culture.
 What documents are needed for age proof?
For proof of age, please attach Birth Certificate/ Ration Card/ 10th Class Certificate.
 What if I am differently abled?
If you are differently abled, please attach the Physical Disability Certificate.
 What are the options for the language preference?
Assamese, Bengali, Bodo, Dogri, Gujarati, Hindi, Kannada, Kashmiri, Konkani, Maithili, Malayalam, Manipuri, Marathi, Nepali, Oriya, Punjabi, Sanskrit, Santhali, Sindhi, Tamil, Telugu and Urdu.
 What is Nomination?
Please select yes, if you have been nominated for the GOAL program by any MP/ MLA, Gol class 1 officers, Head of TRI/ ITDPs District Level, PRI Representatives, Reputed NGOs funded by MoTA, CoEs Corporate Organisation, NGO & other Business Organisation.
 What is NSTFDC or MUDRA?
NSTFDC: National Scheduled Tribes Finance and Development Corporation
MUDRA: Micro Units Development and Refinance Agency Bank
 What is a domicile location?
Domicile location is the place where you currently reside in.

 How will a mentee be appointed to a mentor?
The Mentor-Mentee appointment will happen on the following parameters:
1) The Mentor and Mentee must be fluent in the same language.
2) There should be alignment of area of interests between the mentor and mentee.
